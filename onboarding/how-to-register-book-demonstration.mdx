### Book a demonstration

To learn more about **sharemundo links®**, we encourage our prospective customers to schedule a demonstration with our dedicated team. This option is easily accessible via our website.  To schedule a demonstration, please click on the "Book an Appointment" button on the home page of our website and select a time slot that is convenient for you ([sharemundo.com](https://sharemundo.com/rendez-vous/)).

![](/Captured'écran2025-02-03124617.png)

This 30-minute-long online meeting is dedicated to the introduction of the principal features and functionalities of **sharemundo links®.** It will also be an initial contact with you and your company in so providing an opportunity to determine your needs and see how we can meet your requirements.
